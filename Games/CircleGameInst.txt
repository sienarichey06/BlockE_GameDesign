Goal Of The Game:

CIRCLE goal: chase square around screen to eat it

SQUARE goal: run around screen to avoid being eaten by the circle

Who is the winner?
Square wins IF it catches the square 3 times in 1 minute.
Circle wins IF it avoids the square for 1 minute.
IF circle catches the square once, it is a rematch

KEYS:
circle= up, down, right, left
square= w=up, a=left, s=down, d=right

GOOD LUCK!
